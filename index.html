The hidden risk

When your internet goes down,
the damage is immediate.

It doesn't take a major outage. A cut cable, a router fault, a carrier issue — and your business stops.

💳
Payments stop instantly
Card machines go offline. Every customer who can't pay walks out the door. Revenue stops the second your connection drops.
📵
Calls go unanswered
VoIP phones depend on internet. When it fails, incoming calls drop and new enquiries go straight to a competitor.
🗓️
Systems and bookings fail
Cloud software, booking platforms, appointment systems — all inaccessible. Staff can't work. Customers can't book.
🔒
Security goes dark
Remote CCTV and alarm monitoring lose their connection. You lose visibility of your premises with no warning.
